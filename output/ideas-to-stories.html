Course Modules

A structured learning path designed to take you from beginner to confident AI storyteller.

Module 1: Story Foundations

Learn the fundamentals of storytelling and how AI can enhance your creative process. Understand story structure, character development, and narrative arcs.

Module 2: AI Writing Tools

Master AI-powered writing assistants like ChatGPT and Claude. Learn prompts, editing techniques, and how to maintain your unique voice.

Module 3: Visual Storytelling

Create stunning visuals to accompany your stories using AI image generation tools like Midjourney and DALL-E.

Module 4: Content Strategy

Develop a comprehensive content strategy that leverages AI tools for maximum impact and audience engagement.
